About GFORM Shoes

Gymur Shoes Ltd is a long serving shoeline dating back from May 12th 1974, established by the Wesh Brothers(W.Bros).The shoeline is well distributed intentionally having its headquarters in Nairobi, Kenya's Central Province. It is a renowned brand owing to the positive customer reviews as well as superb recommendations from prestigious health firms such as YogaIt, BrightSide etc. Since our establishment in 1974, we have taken pride in satisfying our customers with what strikes their eyes from downright flashiness to the informal indoor shoe of your liking.
